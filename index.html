Interview Coaching

Ready to Ace Your Next Interview?

Are you a teenager, fresh out of college, or an adult looking to sharpen your interview skills? My sessions are designed to help you gain insight, build confidence, and improve your performance in any interview setting.

  • Personalized Interview Sessions: Tailored to your needs (academic, first job, job change).
  • Adaptive Approach: Keen awareness to different cultures, personality types, and ages.
  • Expert Feedback: Get constructive feedback from an experienced professional.
  • Realistic Practice: Simulate real interview scenarios to reduce anxiety.
  • Comprehensive Preparation: Cover common questions and tricky situations.
Interview Prep Step by Step Guide will help you get started and set the right framework ahead of or in parallel to engaging in a mock interview.

Interview Preparation Checklist is a summary checklist that will help you jot down your thoughts and prepare for your interview.

About Me

With two decades as a hiring manager in companies spanning from start up, education technology, consulting, and large multinational software companies, I bring insider knowledge of what interviewers and general employers are looking for. As a father of three teens and a seasoned coach of boys' and girls' teams in elementary, middle, and high school (baseball, basketball, soccer, and volleyball), I understand the unique challenges and strengths of young adults. My coaching experience has honed my ability to mentor and guide individuals towards achieving their full potential.

Why Choose Me?

  • Experience Across Age Groups: Whether you're a teenager, recent graduate, or seasoned professional, I’ve got you covered.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Convenient times focused on evenings and weekends.

Session Structure

  • 10 minutes introduction and ice breaker (as needed).
  • 30 minutes mock interview.
  • 20 minutes discuss feedback and strategies.
  • With multiple sessions, I am happy to tailor the structure based on your specific need and to focus on specific areas you would like to improve.
